Project Euler 3 - Largest Prime Factor

Aug 22, 2014

Problem 3

We’ve got to find the largest prime factor of a certain number.

This algorithm involves finding the smallest prime factor, dividing the test number by that factor, and continuing on until we’re left with a prime.

The printLPFCalcs(number) function just makes the printing pretty, as you’ll see below.
