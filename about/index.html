About me

I hail from South Jersey, went to school in Boston, and currently live in NYC.

I’m interested in cool stuff - physics, philosophy, programming, economics, math, productivity, games - and want to start writing about all of it.

I’ve got 3 cats, enjoy longboarding, and am recovering from a two-year start-up odyssey.

Unfortunately, I still can’t grow a great beard at 25 years old. It’s pretty lame.

I think Ayn Rand is rad, and any coffee shop pseudo-intellectual hipster who tells you otherwise doesn’t know what he’s talking about.

I’m currently reading Gödel, Escher, Bach by Douglas Hofstadter, and it’s getting me real stoked on AI.

I’m working on a 2011 macbook air and it runs like a dream. Some day I’ll be a true hacker and use linux instead of osx.

Speaking of my computer, I’m enrolled in a Data Science course at General Assembly, which is just great.

I fucking love Settlers of Catan and am making a short-term goal of mocking up a digital version so I can play with my friends. If it ever pans out beyond that, I’ll keep it free and open-source, but have to imagine I’ll get in trouble for making it without their permission. I found at least one promising repo that I could build upon.

I think Kanye West, Macklemore, Kendrick Lamar, and Beyonce are overrated.

It blows my mind that some cafes in this city literally choose to not provide wifi.

My dream car is a Lotus Elise.

Through continuous exposure, I forced myself to like olives and whiskey. You can too!

I think the two great evils of the world are banner ads and click bait. A better web is possible.

I’m really trying to be a super cool hacker.
